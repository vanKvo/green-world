Our Priorities

Tackle Climate Change

Climate change is happening now. It is not a distant threat anymore. The impacts of climate change including more extreme weather and natural disasters, chronic droughts and economic instability have become apparent than ever. Our planet has become hotter than any other time recorded history. Together, we can halt the catastrophic effects of climate change.

Protect Land and Water

Globally, only 5% of the natural lands at high risk of development are protected. We won’t survive without clean water and land. We also need healthy, diverse oceans for fishing, working & playing. Working with governments, corporations, local partners & you, we can protect more nature between today & 2025 than we have in our history.

Healthy Food and Clean Water for All People

Food is sustainable when it benefits the environment and maintains soil fertility. We’re fostering innovations in technology, collaborating with communities to use resources more efficiently and promoting policies that enable sustainability. We can secure healthy food and clean water for all people without sacrificing the environment.
