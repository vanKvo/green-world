About Us

Green World is a globally environmental nonprofit working to create a world where people and nature can thrive. Founded in 1991 in the Unites States, the organization has grown to become a well-known environmental organization in the world. Thanks to more than a million members and the dedicated efforts of our diverse staff and scientists, we impact conservation in many countries and territories across six continents.
